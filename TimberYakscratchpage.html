TimberYak is a venture founded by Adam Shill with his cousin Matthew Mella and good friend Kade Cook

Based out of Wallsburg, Utah, we work to showcase the beauty of mountains and starry skys in west with reclaimed wood, providing any dwelling with a modern - rustic asthetic














Our name is inspired by the family ranch that we are busy working at when we are not creating wood-scapes. With over 2 dozen yaks on sight, we wanted to stay true to our roots and origins














Building the wood-scapes is an intensive process

  1. Aquire Materials

    • Aged Pallets
    • Barn Wood
    • Screws
    • Wood Glue
    • Stain
    • LED string lights
    • Plywood
    • 11 gauge steel

  2. Prepare Materials

    • Disassemble Pallets
    • Cut boards, plywood, and steel brackets
    • Assemble Frame
    • stain plywood

  3. Build out mountain & sky pieces
    • Find the most textured pieces of wood
    • Cut to variety of sizes & angles
    • Glue & stain pieces
    • Install LEDs - if applicable
